Features
Sign In as Teacher/Student
Allows Users to register and access the platform based on their role - Teacher or Student
Create and Join ClassRooms
Teachers can create subject specific classrooms while students can join them using a code or invite.
Assign and Complete Tasks
Teachers can assign task, and students can mark them as completed in their to-do list.
Track Student Progress
Teachers can monitor each student's task completion status and progress over time.
Leaderboard System
Displays a leaderboard based on task completion to encourage healthy competition among Students.
Resource Sharing
Teachers and Students can share study materials, notes, and links within classrooms
Student Feedback
Students can submit their feedback to Teachers about Lessons, tasks, or the overall classroom experince.

About StudySphere
StudySphere is a collaborative learning platform that simplifies virtual classroom management and strengthens teacher-student interaction. Teachers can create subject-specific classrooms, assign tasks, share resources, and track progress, while students complete tasks, access materials, and view their standing on a dynamic leaderboard.
